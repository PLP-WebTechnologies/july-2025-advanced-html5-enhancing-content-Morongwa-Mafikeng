CODING BOOTCAMPS

The idea behind bootcamps is to offer comprehensive learning for people who do not want to go through the institutional route or for other reasons like financial troubles at an offordable price.

These bootcamps are created in a way that is easily accessible and planned by implementing a curriculum that can be followed in a way that is self paced. This is very advantageous for people who are busy with other things like full time jobs, seeking opportunities to enhance their enterpreneural endeavours etc.

All that is needed is:

  1. Internet Access
  2. A working Laptop or Computer
  3. A learning structure to follow
Bootcamps offer different courses for for different people who want to follow a certain career path. An example can be a person striving to be a software engineer. The course may entail learning languages like:
Language Description
Python The outside or skeleton of a web interface
Css The design of web interface
Javascript Interation between the elements of the interface
